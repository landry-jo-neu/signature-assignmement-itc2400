Barcelona, Spain

Things to Do

  • Visit the La Sagrada Família Cathedral
  • Explore the Museu Egipci de Barcelona
  • Take in panoramic views of Barcelona from Montjuïc Castle
  • See the Casa Battló
  • Tour the Casa Padellàs (Barcelona History Museum MUHBA)

Food to Try

  • El Mercat
  • Levante Mediterranean Bistrot
  • Mercat de la Boqueria (La Boqueria)

Transportation

  • Fly into Josep Tarradellas Barcelona–El Prat Airport
  • Cruising into Barcelona Cruise Port
  • Taxis are readily available
  • Many of the major neighborhoods are very walkable
  • Accomodation Recommendation: Cotton House Hotel is an excellent hotel in the Eixample neighborhood
