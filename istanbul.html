Istanbul, Türkiye

Things to Do

  • Visit the Blue Mosque and Hagia Sophia Grand Mosque
  • Explore the Egyptian Bazaar in Mısır Çarşısı
  • Cross the Bosphorus Strait by ferry or by the Bosphorus Bridge that connects the continents of Europe and Asia
  • Tour the Beylerbeyi Palace

Food to Try

  • Galata Rooftop Ocakbaşı Restaurant
  • Karaköy Köşkeroğlu Tophane
  • Tatbak

Transportation

  • Fly into Istanbul Airport
  • Cruising into Galataport İstanbul cruise port
  • Taxis are readily available
  • Sultanahmet district and surrounding areas are walkable
