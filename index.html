Quick Travels with Jonathan

Quick Travels with Jonathan offers insight into making the most of short trips through self-paced itineraries based on my personal travel experiences. Whether you’re planning a long weekend or in between a flight layover, you’ll find some ideas for must-see spots, quality cuisine, and practical tips on navigating each destination with confidence. These trips are designed for independent travelers who prefer exploring without tour groups or guides, prioritizing the flexibility to move at your own pace, discover hidden gems, and create a travel experience that feels authentic and entirely your own.
