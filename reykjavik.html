Reykjavik, Iceland

Things to Do

  • Walk downtown Reykjavik
  • Visit Hallgrímskirkja church
  • Drive the Golden Circle to see some of Iceland's greatest natural sights
  • Relax at Sky Lagoon hot springs
  • Visit Perlan Icelandic Nature Museum

Food to Try

  • BakaBaka
  • Friðheimar
  • Krua Thai
  • Brauð & Co

Transportation

  • Fly into Keflavík International Airport
  • Renting a car is highly recommended to travel from airport and to get anywhere outside of the city
  • Downtown Reykjavik is walkable
  • Accomodation Recommendation: 101 Hotel is a central hotel downtown with parking
